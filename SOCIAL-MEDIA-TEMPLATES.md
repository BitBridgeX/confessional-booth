## 1. INSTAGRAM TEMPLATES

### 1.1 Feed Post (Square)
**Dimensions**: 1080 × 1080 px  
**Format**: PNG or MP4  
**Safe Zone**: 1000 × 1000 px (leave 40px margin)

**Template Variants**:

#### A. Gamification Achievement Post
```
Top section (30%):
  - "LEVEL UP" in Playfair Display (72px, gold)
  - Animated neon glow

Middle section (40%):
  - Achievement badge (120x120px, centered)
  - Pulsing glow animation
  - Dark purple gradient background

Bottom section (30%):
  - User name / achievement (Geist 18px, cream)
  - "Join the ritual 👑" CTA
  - QR code or link (optional)

Copy Examples:
- "Sister Mary hit LEVEL 50 🔥 Are you ready to confess?"
- "SAINT UNLOCKED ✨ Join 50K+ sinners & seekers"
```

#### B. Confession Snippet Post
```
Design:
  - Quoted text over dark background
  - Hot pink neon border
  - Blur/anonymize personal details
  - Electric blue flourishes

Copy:
  "The hardest part isn't admitting the sin.
   It's admitting you loved it." — Anonymous Confessor
  
Footer: "Your story. Your way. HOLYFANS."
```

#### C. Merch Product Post
```
Layout:
  - Product image (center, 60% of canvas)
  - Gold frame/border
  - Product name in Playfair (36px, gold)
  - Price in Geist (24px, cream)
  - "Link in bio" or button overlay
  - Lifestyle styling (sensual, dark)

Copy:
  "AMEN, SLUT — Now available
   Premium cotton, neon-pink print"
```

### 1.2 Instagram Stories (Vertical)
**Dimensions**: 1080 × 1920 px  
**Format**: PNG sequence (animated)  
**Duration**: 5-15 seconds per story

**Template Variants**:

#### A. Daily Sin Meter
```
Frame 1: Static title "YOUR SIN METER"
Frame 2-4: Animated meter fill (purple → gold → pink)
Frame 5: Final value with CTA sticker
  "How sinful are YOU?"
```

#### B. Poll Story
```
Full screen background: Dark gradient
Question: Large Playfair text (48px, gold)
Poll options: Two hot pink buttons
Footer: "Tap to confess →"

Examples:
- "Have you EVER lied to get ahead?"
- "Which penance calls to you?"
```

#### C. Achievement Unlock
```
Animation sequence:
  Frame 1: Locked badge (grayscale)
  Frame 2: Unlock effect (scale up, particles)
  Frame 3: Badge with glow
  Frame 4: "You unlocked: [ACHIEVEMENT]"
  Frame 5: CTA sticker "Claim your power"
```

### 1.3 Reels (Video)
**Dimensions**: 1080 × 1920 px or 1920 × 1080 px  
**Duration**: 15, 30, 60 seconds  
**Format**: MP4, codec H.264

**Content Ideas**:
1. **User Confessions** (15-30s)
   - Anonymous voice-over
   - Moody footage montage
   - Neon text overlays
   - Final frame: "Confess. Seek. Grow."

2. **Achievement Showcases** (30s)
   - User hitting new level
   - Celebrate with effects
   - Show rank progression
   - CTA: "Reach this level"

3. **Penance Challenges** (30-60s)
   - Week-long penance activity
   - Montage of participation
   - Leaderboard moment
   - "Join the challenge"

## 9. DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS (All Platforms)

### Colors (Standard Hex Codes)
```
Purple: #2D1B4E
Gold: #D4AF37
Hot Pink: #FF1493
Electric Blue: #00D9FF
Black: #0a0a0a
Cream: #F5F1E8
```

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display Bold, 600-700 weight
- **Body**: Geist Regular, 400 weight
- **Accent**: IBM Plex Mono (tech, stats)
- **Min size**: 14px (readable on small screens)

### Visual Effects
- Glow/shadow on hot pink elements: `0 0 20px rgba(255, 20, 147, 0.4)`
- Neon text shadow: Layered 3x with decreasing opacity
- Hover effects: Scale + glow on interactive elements
